DEVIATIONS & DEVIANTS

DEVIATIONS

There is something inexplicable on this planet—the Deviations. They defy logic and possess supernatural powers beyond life, time, and thermodynamics. Most are animated and sentient.

TERRITORY

They help Meta-Humans look after their territories, taking over daily tasks so the Meta-Humans can explore the world more freely.

COMBAT

They fight alongside Meta-Humans, providing support in battles and world exploration. They can expose enemies' weak points or transform into walls to block projectiles.

CRAFTING

They provide Meta-Humans with enhancement potions, tactical items, or peculiar gadgets. Drink a whimsical potion and turn into a chair that hides in plain sight.

DEVIANTS

Most creatures affected by Stardust transform into Deviants, while few can become Meta-Humans. Deviants aren't aggressive at first before being overcome by primordial instincts, turning savage and brutal.

WANDERER

The "Wanderers" tirelessly travel along their paths, as if reenacting every ordinary day before the Starfall. They have no destination, nor can they find their way back.

RAVENOUS HUNTER

Devour everything, possess everything. Life itself will be transformed into an eternal gluttony!

TREANT

Return peacefully to nature's warm embrace and feel the cruel compassion of Gaia, the mother of all things, and the blessing of all souls!

SHADOW HOUND

Chaser of the shadows, all that is flesh and blood is yours to dominate. Life itself will be offered up to you.

FORSAKEN GIANT

Is forgetting really such a bad thing? Perhaps for those who have been forgotten, it's the act of remembrance that truly hurts.
